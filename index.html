NICKOLA TESLA

  • 1856 - Born July 10, in Smiljan, Croatia
  • 1870 - Gimnazija Karlovac high school
  • 1874 - Tesla evaded conscription into the Austro-Hungarian Army in Smiljan by running away
  • 1875 - Graz University of Technology
  • 1878 - Nickola wasn't enough of a hero; one biographer suggests Tesla wasn't studying and may have been expelled for gambling and womanizing!?!
  • 1881 - moved to Budapest, Hungary he worked as a draftsman in the Central Telegraph Office
  • 1882 - Tesla moved to Ivry-sur-Seine suburb of Paris starting working at Continental Edison Company. senting him on to troubleshoot engineering problems at other Edison utilities being built around France and in Germany.
  • 1884 - Emigrated to the United States, where he became a naturalized citizen
  • 1884 - proceeds to change the world and facilitates the modern world as we know it!!
  • Died: January 7, 1943, The New Yorker Hotel, New York, NY USA
